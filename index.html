The Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ

For our exhortation does not come from error or impurity or by way of deceit; but just as we have been approved by God to be entrusted with the gospel, so we speak, not as pleasing men, but God who examines our hearts;

1 Thessalonians 2:3-4

Prerequisites

  • You know you're a sinner
  • God exists
  • You're facing judgment
  • You long to confess your sins and entrust yourself to God through the Lord Jesus Christ

Sin

and He said to them, “Thus it is written, that the Christ would suffer and rise again from the dead the third day, and that repentance for forgiveness of sins would be proclaimed in His name to all the nations, beginning from Jerusalem. Then He opened their minds to understand the Scriptures, You are witnesses of these things.

Luke 24:45-48

As my poor understanding allows me to speak, I would say that we all are sinners. None is good save God. God is Good, and we are evil. It is easy to recognise our condition as wrongdoers. Our conscience testifies that we're guilty.
